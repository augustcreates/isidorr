FAQ · よくある質問

Promoter answers, fast.

The questions venues ask before they hold a date — answered in advance.

What's the standard tech rider?

Two CDJs (3000 / Nexus2 / Nexus3) and a DJM mixer is the baseline. Beat-live sets add a sampler and a stereo channel. Vinyl on request — needs two working turntables with sharp needles.

How fast can a date be confirmed?

Inside Tokyo: a confirmed offer (date, fee, format, curfew) gets a yes / no inside 24 hours. Travel dates: 48–72h to coordinate logistics.

Will you travel outside Tokyo?

Yes — Osaka, Kyoto, Yokohama, Nagoya all welcome. International routing handled for lineups with at least four weeks of notice.

Set length flexibility?

Beat-live sets sit cleanest at 30 min. DJ support slots flex from 45 to 90 min, and a producer+MC night takes a full 60–90 min slot with the lineup planned.

What does a clean booking note look like?

Venue, capacity, date, set length, fee or fee range, lineup, sound system, curfew. Use the form below — it copies a brief in that exact shape.
